Let’s Learn How to Make a Delicious Veg Sandwich

Ingrediants used

  • Bread slices
  • Tomatoes
  • Cucumbers
  • Cheese
  • Butter
  • Green chutney

Follow these simple steps to prepare your tasty sandwich:

  1. Take two slices of bread and apply butter on both sides.
  2. Spread a thin layer of green chutney on one slice.
  3. Place tomato, cucumber, and cheese slices evenly.
  4. Cover with the second slice and toast for 3–5 minutes.
  5. Cut diagonally and serve hot!

I love sandwiches because they are quick to make, healthy, and perfect for any time of the day. With just a few ingredients, we can create something delicious and satisfying!
